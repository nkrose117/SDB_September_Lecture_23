Flex Heading

Accomplish

Horizontal or Vertical arrangement of elements

Space distribution of elements

Quick Notes

It is one-dimensional. This will either going to affect a row or column, but not both at the same time.

Useful for layout on nav bars, headers, image galleries

Place Elements that are direct children, but does not affect further children

Flex Container

Our element with the `display: flex` style. This style applies to the container and it's children

Justify Content

Position content along the axis of our flex container

  1. flex-start
  2. flex-end
  3. center
  4. space-between
  5. space-around
  6. space-evenly

Align Items

Similar to Justify Content but works along the cross-axis (perpendicular to the flex-direction)

  1. flex-start
  2. flex-end
  3. center
  4. stretch
  5. baseline
